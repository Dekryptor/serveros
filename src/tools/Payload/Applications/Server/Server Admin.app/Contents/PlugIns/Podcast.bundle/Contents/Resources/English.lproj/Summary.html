Podcast Producer has been configured and started. Here's how to use it.

Podcast Capture

Capture and submit video, audio, and dual-source episodes with Podcast Capture (part of Mac OS X 10.5 or later) or the Podcast Capture web application.

Podcast Library

Browse, stream, and download podcast episodes. Subscribe to any podcast feed in the library with iTunes and automatically sync it with your iPod, iPhone, or Apple TV.

Podcast Composer

Customize the look and feel of your podcasts by, for example, adding a unique watermark or intro video. Podcast Composer also lets you integrate workflows with iTunes U, wikis, and other publishing destinations.
