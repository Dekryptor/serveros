Managing iChat Service

Use the iChat pane to turn iChat instant messaging service on or off, enable chatting with other instant messaging systems, or set up logging and archiving of all chats.

To configure the iChat pane:

  1. Change the desired settings, using the following information as a guide:

    • On/Off: 
      Indicates the status of iChat service, and clicking the On/Off switch turns the service on or off.
    • Enable server-to-server communication: 
      Lets users chat with users on other XMPP instant messaging systems such as Google Talk. For instructions, see Chatting with Users of Google Talk and Other XMPP Services.
    • Log and archive all chats: 
      Saves a transcript of all chats (instant messages) in a file, and archives the transcript once a week in a compressed file. The weekly transcript and compressed archives are saved in /var/jabberd/message_archives/. For instructions, see Saving and Archiving Instant Messages.

  2. Restart iChat service by clicking On/Off twice.

    Changes to iChat service settings don’t take effect until iChat service is restarted.
